The Gem Collector

1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

2. There are four gems below. By clicking on a gem you will add points to your total score.

3. You win the game by matching your total score to a random number provided, you will lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

4. The value of each gem is hidden from you until you click on it.

5. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of the gems.
